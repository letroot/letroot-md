# Compute Constraints

The bottleneck in modern artificial intelligence development and deployment caused by the limited availability of processing power (primarily GPUs and TPUs).

## Overview
As AI models scale up in parameters and usage (especially with agentic reasoning models that consume vast amounts of tokens in the background), the demand for compute has vastly outstripped supply. 

## Business Implications
- **Opportunity Costs**: Cloud providers (hyperscalers) cannot serve all demand, forcing them to prioritize higher-margin internal workloads over external customers.
- **Pricing Power**: AI labs that are compute-constrained (like Anthropic) may restrict access to their most advanced models to maintain pricing power and manage load.
- **Strategic Moats**: Companies like OpenAI view massive, proactive infrastructure build-outs as a primary competitive advantage to capture market share over rivals.
