# Summary
In this episode of the [[Pleros Podcast]], [[Pastor Akim]] continues the series on [[Healing]] in the [[Newness of Life]]. The core message focuses on the distinction between [[Redemption]] making healing available and [[Faith]] being the mechanism that effects it. The teaching explores various dimensions of faith—including the faith of the recipient, third parties, and the often-overlooked responsibility of the [[Minister's Faith]]. Using the examples of the epileptic boy in [[Mark 9]] and the lame man at the Gate Beautiful in [[Acts 3]], the episode emphasizes that healing is a settled reality in the [[Gospel]] to be received by the design of faith.

# Key Insights
- [[Healing]] is a present-hour benefit of the [[Newness of Life]], available in every aspect except for final [[Immortality]] and the incorruption of matter.
- While [[Redemption]] provides the "right" to be healed, it does not automatically effect it; it must be walked in, much like [[Christian Character]].
- There are three primary "fountains" of [[Faith]] mentioned in scripture for healing: the recipient, third parties (friends/family), and the minister.
- The failure to see healing can sometimes be attributed to the [[Minister's Faith]] or [[Unbelief]], as seen in Jesus' rebuke of his disciples in [[Matthew 17]].
- [[Faith]] is not an arbitrary hurdle or a "work" but is [[God's Design]] for the receiving process in the cosmos.

# Coach's Corner
- Stop viewing [[Faith]] as a barrier to your [[Healing]] and start seeing it as the simple "receiving process" designed by God.
- When ministering to others, take responsibility for your own [[Conviction]] in the [[Name of Jesus]] rather than always placing the burden of faith on the sick individual.
- Recognize that [[Supernatural Healing]] is a legitimate defense and a joint response to sickness, even alongside [[Natural Healing]].

# Action Items
- Study the account of the epileptic boy in [[Mark 9]] and [[Matthew 17]] to understand the rebuke of [[Unbelief]].
- Meditate on [[Acts 3:16]] regarding how [[Faith]] in the [[Name of Jesus]] provides "perfect soundness."
- Review the previous episodes on the [[Inner Man]] and [[Mind Renewal]] to provide context for how we grow into this authority.
