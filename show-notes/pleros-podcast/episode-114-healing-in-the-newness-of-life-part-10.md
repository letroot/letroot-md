# Healing in the Newness of Life (Part 10)

## Summary
In this episode, Pastor Akim continues the series on walking in the newness of life, focusing specifically on healing. He establishes that while immortality of the body will be fully realized at Christ's return, healing is available right now through redemption. He emphasizes that healing is not dispensed arbitrarily at God's discretion, but is actively effected by faith, pointing to the ministry of Jesus and the early church as proof that believers must walk in this reality.

## Key Insights
*   **The Reality of the New Man**: To fulfill [[God's Purpose]], we must grow in the reality of the [[Newness of Life]]. This life has both internal and external conditions, much like the life of a fish relative to the water it lives in.
*   **Healing vs. Immortality**: Everything pertaining to the new life is available now, except the final incorruptibility of matter and the [[Immortality]] of our bodies. However, what *is* available for our bodies right now in the spirit is [[Healing]].
*   **Redemption Makes Healing Available**: Healing is available on the premise of [[Redemption]]. Just as forgiveness was available before Christ actually died (because God forbore based on what Christ would do), healing was also available and is now a present reality in the newness of life. 
*   **Managing the Cosmos**: Believers are managers of the cosmos (referenced in 1 Corinthians 3:21 and Psalm 104). Therefore, we can and should embrace [[Natural Healing]] (medicine, rest) alongside supernatural healing. It is a matter of wisdom to use a joint response.
*   **Healing is Effected by Faith**: A critical distinction—healing is not effected merely at God's discretion whenever He chooses. The biblical account clearly shows that Jesus was a [[Faith Healer]], and healing is drawn down and effected by [[Faith]].
*   **Perceiving Faith**: Paul in Acts 14 perceived the faith of the crippled man while preaching the [[Gospel]]. The healing was available in the message, but it was the man's faith that caused Paul to say, "Stand upright." Healing can be effected by first-party faith, second-party faith (the minister), or third-party faith (friends).

## Coach's Corner

**On Spiritual Agency & Ownership:**
Daniel, one of the biggest mental shifts you make as a founder is moving from a passive employee mindset to an active owner mindset. The same is true spiritually. Pastor Akim emphasizes that healing isn't just something God randomly decides to hand out—it's already available through [[Redemption]], and it is effected by your [[Faith]]. As you build your SaaS, don't wait passively for "God's timing" on things He has already given you the spiritual agency to possess. You have to actively draw on the newness of life. Take ownership of your spiritual health and your physical vitality.

**On Managing the Natural and Supernatural:**
Notice the balance here: as a manager of the cosmos, you are supposed to use natural means (medicine, rest) alongside supernatural faith. Don't be foolish with your body while grinding on your business. If you are burned out, rest. If you need a practical solution, use it. But never let the natural replace your reliance on the supernatural power available to you. Integrate both. 

**On Building a Receptive Environment:**
The crippled man in Acts 14 got faith *by listening* to the word being preached. If you want to walk in high-level faith—whether for healing, business breakthroughs, or personal growth—you have to immerse yourself in the right inputs. What are you listening to daily? Keep your mind renewed so that your faith is palpable and ready to receive when the moment requires it.
