# Healing in the Newness of Life (Part 11)

In this episode of the [[Pleros Podcast]], Pastor Akim explores the practical application of [[Supernatural Healing]] within the [[Newness of Life]]. The discussion shifts from the availability of healing to the mechanism of its manifestation: [[Faith]]. By examining various New Testament accounts, the teaching demonstrates that healing is not a matter of [[God's Discretion]] alone but is "affected" by the [[Faith]] of the recipient, the minister, or even third parties. The episode emphasizes that the [[Gospel]] provides a [[Sense of Right]] to health that believers must actively lay hold of.

# Key Insights

- [[Healing]] is the reality of the [[World to Come]] made available to our bodies today, bridging the gap between our current state and future [[Immortality]].
- There is a vital distinction between [[Natural Healing]] (harnessing the [[Cosmos]] through [[Medicine]] and [[Wisdom]]) and [[Supernatural Healing]] (effected by the [[Power of God]]).
- The [[Preaching of the Gospel]] is designed to stir a [[Sense of Right]] to healing, allowing the listener to reckon that they can lay hold of God's power.
- [[Faith]] is the consistent variable in the ministry of [[Jesus]] and the [[Church]] for affecting the available [[Power of God]].
- [[Unbelief]], whether in the recipient or the [[Minister]], can act as a barrier to the "mighty works" God intends to perform.

# Coach's Corner

- Avoid the "discretionary mindset" which views healing as a lottery; instead, cultivate a [[Scriptural Foundation]] that views health as part of your [[Redemptive Rights]].
- [[Persistence]] in [[Faith]], as seen in the [[Syrophoenician Woman]], is not about begging God but about being so convinced of one's right to the [[Children's Bread]] that one refuses to be denied.
- Recognize that [[Supernatural Healing]] serves as a [[Defense]], a [[Joint Response]], and sometimes the [[Only Option]] in the face of sickness.

# Action Items

- Review [[Acts 14]] and [[Matthew 8]] to observe how [[Faith]] was identified and commended by [[Paul]] and [[Jesus]].
- Audit your internal dialogue regarding sickness to ensure you are moving from "hope" to the active [[Reckoning]] of [[Faith]].
- Study the concept of [[Daily Bread]] as it relates to [[Healing]] being a daily provision for the believer.
