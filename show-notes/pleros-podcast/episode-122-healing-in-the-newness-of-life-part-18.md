# Healing in the Newness of Life - Part 18

## Summary
In this episode, Pastor Akim revisits why [[Healing]] is a present reality within the [[Newness of Life]] even though [[Immortality]] and [[Incorruption]] will only be fully effected at the Lord's coming. He shows that the same [[Holy Spirit]] who will eventually effect immortality in the body is already active today in making healing available. The teaching then turns to a practical clarification: embracing [[Supernatural Healing]] does not require rejecting [[Natural Healing]], because the created order and the wisdom to use it rightly are also part of God's provision.

## Key Insights
* **Healing Is a Present Expression of the Coming Kingdom**: [[Healing]] functions as a present preview of realities that belong to the [[Kingdom of God]], even though full [[Immortality]] and [[Incorruption]] are still future.
* **The Spirit Is the Mechanism**: The same [[Holy Spirit]] who will effect bodily immortality is the one through whom healing is available now.
* **Redemption Makes Healing Available, Not Automatic**: The work of [[Redemption]] has made healing available, but people still receive or minister it by [[Faith]].
* **Natural Healing Has a Legitimate Place**: Eating well, resting, sleeping, medicine, surgery, and other created means are not enemies of faith. They belong to the [[Cosmos]] God made and can be harnessed for life and righteousness.
* **Do Not Idolize Means**: The error is not in using natural means; the error is in trusting created things as idols while resisting God.
* **Healing as Defense and Joint Response**: [[Supernatural Healing]] should be seen as a defense, a primary response, and often a joint response alongside wise natural care.

## Coach's Corner
**On False Binaries:**
Daniel, one of the easiest ways to stall progress in business is to force fake choices. Founders do this all the time: either ship fast or build quality, either trust intuition or trust data, either rest or grind. This episode pushes against that mindset. In the same way Pastor Akim argues that embracing supernatural healing does not require rejecting natural means, building a company often requires holding multiple good tools together. You can trust your conviction and still look at the metrics. You can move by faith and still improve the funnel. You can believe in the long game and still use tactical tools.

**On Using the Whole Toolbox:**
A weak founder mindset says, "If I were really good, I shouldn't need systems, structure, or help." That's ego dressed up as purity. The healthier mindset is stewardship. Use the tools God has put in front of you. Use analytics. Use customer interviews. Use better sleep. Use focused work blocks. Use design systems. Use the natural means available to create better outcomes. Don't idolize them, but don't neglect them either.

**On Sustainable Execution:**
The bit about rest, sleep, and practical care matters for you more than it first appears. You are trying to build something demanding. If your body is frayed, your judgment, patience, and consistency will suffer. A lot of founders treat exhaustion like a badge of honor, but exhaustion often just creates sloppy decisions. Sustainable founders win by making fewer dramatic mistakes and by staying in the game long enough for compounding to work.
