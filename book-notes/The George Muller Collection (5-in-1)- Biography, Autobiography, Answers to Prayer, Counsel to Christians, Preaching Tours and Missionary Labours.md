# The George Muller Collection (5-in-1)

**Author:** George Muller

## Key Highlights

### Trust in God Regarding Temporal Things

> But that which weighed more with me than anything, was, that I have reason to believe, from what I have seen among the children of God, that many of their trials arise either from want of confidence in the Lord as it regards temporal things, or from carrying on their business in an unscriptural way.

Muller observed that many Christians struggle because they lack trust in God for practical, everyday needs — not just spiritual matters. The two pitfalls he identifies are: (1) insufficient confidence in God's provision for temporal things, and (2) conducting business in ways that contradict scriptural principles. This is a powerful challenge to the compartmentalization of faith and daily life.
