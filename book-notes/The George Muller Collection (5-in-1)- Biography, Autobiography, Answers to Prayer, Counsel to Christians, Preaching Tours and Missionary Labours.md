# The George Muller Collection (5-in-1)

**Author:** George Muller

## The Root of Trials: Lack of Trust in Temporal Things

Muller observed that many of the trials Christians face stem from a lack of confidence in God's provision for their daily, practical needs — or from conducting business in ways that don't align with Scripture. The spiritual life and the material life are not separate compartments; faith must govern both.

> But that which weighed more with me than anything, was, that I have reason to believe, from what I have seen among the children of God, that many of their trials arise either from want of confidence in the Lord as it regards temporal things, or from carrying on their business in an unscriptural way.
