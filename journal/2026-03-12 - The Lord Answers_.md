# The Lord Answers?
I woke up feeling great. Hopeful

I’ve lacked hope for weeks now.  I need hope in great amounts Daniel. I need a lot of hope. A lot of hope. Would Perez’s work ever be done? Would I ever get the free time to work on eCommOS as I should.

## The Problem of Evil
See in Deut. 30 and Genesis 2 that you can choose how you turn out; God sets life or death before man. But what chance did I have as a 12 year old before the pull of porn. Even when I got fervent for the Lord 3 years later at 15 years old. I’d pray 4 hours every Saturday, I’d talk to Uncle Akin about my habit and yet no change.

How do you explain that? It does seem that it’s whenever the truth’s influence wins that Life really begins. I didn’t know how to beat an addiction. I just didn’t know. If it was about praying, I was praying. I didn’t know what Faith Stand was. I find that very unfair. I think we should normalise talking about the unfairness of the reign of sin and death.

I think my anger toward God is that He maybe could have tried harder, made it easier for me to walk.

But the answer seems to be: Yes! We’re all being affected by this tyrannical reign. Don’t over personalise your problems. 

I’m just really jealous of Pastor. Why did he have that early drive and I didn’t. His steps just so aligned to be in the right place at the right time. He had the right attitude too. 

I guess there can’t be an even distribution of these things. You have to look at examples of people who spent decades in jail and came out to be mightily used of God or Smith Wigglesworth who didn’t have an education and move the power of God so mightily. 

You have to take your disadvantages and offer them up to the Lord in humility, He too knowing that the flesh has robbed you so much, and He would make and mould it into His purpose and will for your life.

You have to believe that GOD HAS A PLAN FOR MY LIFE. You have to believe this. And you know you don’t. You’re so anxious that you think God doesn’t even really think about you nor does He have great things He would use you for. So you think you have to do all the heavy lifting.

You need to snap out of  this, it’s just wrong.

## Obeying the Lord \(CU\)
In 2019, I began to seek the Lord. I’d set out as early as 5 am to pray behind the health centre asking the Lord to show me what’s next in His plan for my life. I paraphrase. This was a prompting I obeyed because my heart was open to Him. That led to me attending LWC that year.

This is an example of making the choice for life. But why did I make the choice? The state of my heart. So we can’t choose who we are, but we can choose the influences that would shape us into who we want to be. Hmm

So I can by deciding the influence around me, choose who I turn out to be. What if I don’t feel like choosing the influence of light? What if darkness has reigned so much in my heart that the sound of truth is something I’m so hardened toward? I don’t think there are easy answers to these questions. 

I should just face my own transformation. 

## What to do now
My heart is clearly hardened toward the Lord. It’s really bad. I don’t believe that He can move in my life. That He would work the miraculous through me, etc. I’m so sold that He has this vendetta against me, that I’m on some blacklist or something. It’s bad. I need to fix this first. How? Fasting; much reading and fasting and praying,
