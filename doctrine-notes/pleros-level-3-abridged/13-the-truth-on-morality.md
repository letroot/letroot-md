## Morality as a Contentious Subject

We're looking at the truth on morality. Morality is a highly contentious subject matter, both within the faith of Christ and outside it. People will always say, to you it is wrong, to me it is not, so the core issue becomes whether morality is subjective or objective.

Morality is on a different pedestal because we have responses to give to anybody and everybody on it, no matter the worldview. And one of the big follow-up questions is this: if someone says what is right is whatever God says, is it right because he said it, or does God say it because it is right? Scripture does not leave us in the dark on these matters.

So the discussion is not small. We are dealing with subjectivity and objectivity, but also with the grounds of morality itself. Is there a truth of morality at all, or are we only talking about preferences, feelings, customs, and agreements? Scripture is saying there is a truth of morality.
