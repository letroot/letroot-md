# Chapters & Topics

## Conversational e-commerce overview

Inioluwa outlined a conversational e-commerce flow where AI consults product data, confirms availability and pricing, prompts payment, and routes orders for delivery. The architecture includes front office (conversation) and back office (routing and delivery) components. The product vision is an AI that handles ordering, pricing, payment, and routing to fulfillment systems. The solution depends on merchants having structured data.

## Data requirements and Nigerian market feasibility

The discussion focused on the dependency of AI on structured merchant data, with Inioluwa warning that many Nigerian businesses may not have accessible systems. If data is captured and current, AI can check availability and pricing in real time. Many Nigerian small businesses may lack documentation, blocking AI automation. The system should connect to multiple data sources and use prompts for outputs.

## Technical approach and prompts

Participants described a technical approach where AI connects to multiple sources and executes prompts to reply, calculate costs, and guide payments. Inioluwa suggested building connectors and prompt templates to automate responses.
