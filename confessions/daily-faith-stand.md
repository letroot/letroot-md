# Daily Faith Stand Confession

## 1. Redemption & New Creation Reality
I am a new creation in Christ Jesus. I am the righteousness of God in Christ. I am born of God; therefore, I overcome the world. Sin has no dominion over me. I am dead to sin and alive to God. I have been delivered from the power of darkness and translated into the Kingdom of God's dear Son.

## 2. Divine Life & Health
The law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus has made me free from the law of sin and death. The same Spirit that raised Jesus from the dead dwells in me and quickens my mortal body. Divine health is my portion. Sickness and disease have no place in my body. I walk in vitality and strength every day.

## 3. Divine Intelligence & Wisdom
I have the mind of Christ. I function with supernatural intelligence. The wisdom of God is at work in me, directing my path and ordering my steps. I know what to do at every point in time. I am not confused; I have clarity of purpose and direction.

## 4. Supernatural Provision & Favor
The Lord is my Shepherd; I shall not want. God supplies all my needs according to His riches in glory by Christ Jesus. I am blessed in my going out and in my coming in. Favor surrounds me like a shield. I am a magnet for divine opportunities and resources.

## 5. Maximum Productivity & Purpose
I am fruitful in every good work. I am productive in my natural and spiritual assignments. I do not labor in vain. The work of my hands is blessed. I am fulfilling God's purpose for my life with excellence and speed. I am bearing fruit that remains.

## 6. Protection & Victory
No weapon formed against me shall prosper. Every tongue that rises against me in judgment is condemned. I dwell in the secret place of the Most High and abide under the shadow of the Almighty. A thousand may fall at my side, and ten thousand at my right hand, but it shall not come near me. I am more than a conqueror through Him who loved me.

## 7. The Power of Words
I hold fast the confession of my faith without wavering. My words are spirit and they are life. I speak life to every situation. I decree a thing and it is established unto me. I have what I say. I am a victor, not a victim.

**In Jesus' Name, Amen.**
